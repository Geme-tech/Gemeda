Featured Dishes

Chicken Mandi

The word "Mandi" comes from the Arabic word 'nada', meaning dew, and actually describes the moist 'dewy' texture of meat. Mandi is made from rice, meat (lamb or chicken), and a mixture of mild but intense spices.

Kitfo

Kitfo is an Ethiopian traditional dish which originated among the Gurage people. It consists of minced raw beef, marinated in mitmiita and niter kibbeh.

Ethiopian Tibs

Ethiopian tibs is a dish made from cubed beef or lamb peppers, veggies, lemon Juice, and other ingeridients. It flavorful and delicious meal that is gaining popularity worldwide. Beef tibs recipe is made from beef as the protein, while Ethiopian lamb tibs will use lamb.
